ABOUT THE ARTIST

I have met some great people, but if I am being truthful, I prefer cats. I think we can all learn from cats: they don’t worry about the impression they leave with others, they reveal deep loyalty in nuanced ways, and they do no harm. I enjoy painting cats and would be happy to paint yours. If you would like me to paint your dog, I will reimagine your dog as a cat, and then paint that. I don’t only paint cats, because I like to think that cats enjoy a variety of artistic styles and muses; but when I am not painting cats I am painting for cats.
